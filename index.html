A Step Deeper...

This project is targeted upon carbon emissions duw to automobiles auch as cars. Furthermore, analysing involvement and contribution of various components of automobiles such as engine size, number of cylinders, fuel type and amount of fuel consumption in different scenarios.


Relevant dataset has been collected for analysis. Analysis focuses on general trends following frequency of several factors and comparison with carbon emission factor. For every category, a linear regression model is implemented that gives a prediction based on available data to suggest a trend with certain accuracy.
